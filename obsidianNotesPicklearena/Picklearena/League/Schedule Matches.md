When league have the needed amount of Teams participant the admin can schedule a matches, by click the corresponding button.

![[photo_2026-03-06_10-38-28.jpg]]

Once the button clicked user needs to mention the following info:
- Sheduling Type (Scheduled, Flexible)
Once Scheduled Type is selected the following fields are required:
- Match Format (Single, Best of Three)
- Games per Week (1, 2, 3, 4 games per week)
- Season length (2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12 weeks)
- Max matches for team per day (1, 2, 3 per day)
- Preferred playable days (Any day of the week, Weekdays, Weekends, Mon/Wed/Fri, Tue/Thu)
- Preferred time window (Any time, Morning, Midday, Afternoon, Evening, Late Night, Custom Time)
- Start of the matches (Date picked opened)

![[photo_2026-03-06_10-38-31.jpg]]

Once Flexible Type is picked the following fields are required:
- Games per Round (1, 2, 3, 4 games)
- Total rounds (2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12 rounds)
- Round deadline (3 days, 5 days, 1 week, 10 days, 2 weeks per round)
- Start of the matches (Date picker opened)

![[photo_2026-03-06_10-51-06.jpg]]

After clicking Continue button the user is redirected to the second step of matches scheduling where they can:
- Check the bracket of matches
- Regenerate matches (This will shuffle teams, dates and time)
- Edit Date and Time for specific match
- Confirm the match schedule
