Users can join or create a Team. [[Team Creation]]
Team can be searched by using search input field.

Team can be filtered by the following criterias:
- Location range
- [[Skill Level]]
- [[Age of opponent]]

The user have possibility to navigate to the following tabs:

- My teams
- All teams

By clicking on the team the user is redirected to the [[Team's page]]
